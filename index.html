About Us

We are dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating stray dogs in Bangalore. Our mission is to provide them with shelter, medical care, and find them loving families.


Our Services

  • Rescue and Rehabilitation of stray dogs
  • Medical Care and Vaccination
  • Adoption and Foster Care Programs
  • Community Awareness and Education
  • Volunteer and Donation Opportunities

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a compassionate community where every stray dog is given a safe and loving home, ensuring no dog suffers from neglect or abuse.

Join us for special days filled with fun, learning, and bonding with our rescued pets. These events help socialize the dogs and educate the community on responsible pet ownership.
